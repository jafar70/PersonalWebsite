Summer Placement - Mann’s Solutions

Mann’s Solutions is an established boutique consulting firm that provides bespoke immigration, business and investment services to clients from all over the globe. Their experienced team of consultants deliver a one-to-one service that diligently places your needs and long-term success at the heart of everything we do.

Mann’s Solutions hired me as a Front-end Web Developer intern to design, build, and launch their second website Manns-soultions.com. Mann’s Solutions wanted a clean, minimal site that would allow their users to find which visa they should apply for and receive enquiries from potential clients. My other responsibilities were to gather and refine requirements based on my line manager’s needs. To achieve this, I designed and developed a dynamic landing page which had included web contact forms so the user can send enquiries and included features like text, images, JQuery plugins to present relevant information to the visitors.

The new and responsive Mann’s Solutions(.com) website was launched July 2016.

Technologies I used:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Bootstrap
  • Python (Flask)
