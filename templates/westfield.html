A Level Coursework - Multimedia Application

During my final year of sixth form I undertook a yearlong multimedia application project. I chose to make a multimedia application about for Westfield shopping centre because I am familiar with the shopping centre and I thought creating an application for a shopping centre would be challenging. Before creating the multimedia application, I had to investigate how to create a multimedia application as I had no programming experience at that time. Therefore, I decided to use a piece of software called Motion Studio 3D which allowed you to create an application without needing to know programming. I project was completed on time and within the agreed scope. During the project I gained multimedia skills such as video & audio editing and I understood programming concepts like variables and expressions.
